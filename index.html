What is a Picker Wheel?

A picker wheel, also known as a random name picker or a spinning wheel, is a digital tool that helps you make random choices from a list of options. You enter your choices (like names, tasks, or numbers), and with a click of a button, the wheel spins and randomly lands on one of the options.

These wheels are perfect for:

  • Choosing a winner for a giveaway.
  • Deciding what to eat for dinner.
  • Picking a student to answer a question in class.
  • Making any random decision fun and fair!
